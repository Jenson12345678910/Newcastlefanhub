Alan Shearer vs Everton (2002)

Thunderbolt volley from outside the box into the top corner — a true Shearer classic. Alan Shearer also won the Premier League Golden Boot three times in his career.

Philippe Albert vs Manchester United (1996)

An outrageous chipped finish over Peter Schmeichel in the 5–0 win — one of the club’s most iconic goals. Philippe Albert was a fan favourite for his attacking style as a defender.

Hatem Ben Arfa vs Bolton (2012)

A mesmerizing solo run beating multiple defenders before slotting home. Ben Arfa was known for unpredictable flair and skill.

Jonas Gutierrez vs West Ham (2015)

A dramatic survival goal to help Newcastle avoid relegation, full of passion and determination. Jonas survived testicular cancer before scoring that famous goal.
