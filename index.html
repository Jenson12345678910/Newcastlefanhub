Newcastle United Owners

Current Owners: Newcastle United is currently owned by the Public Investment Fund of Saudi Arabia (PIF), who completed a takeover in October 2021. They hold 80% of the club, while PCP Capital Partners and RB Sports & Media share the remaining 20%.

Previous Owner: Mike Ashley (2007–2021) oversaw the club with mixed results, stabilizing its finances but frustrating fans with limited investment.

Notable Past Owner: Sir John Hall (1992–1997), who is fondly remembered for backing Kevin Keegan’s “Entertainers” era, taking the club back to the Premier League and competing for titles.
